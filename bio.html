Bio

Born in 1996, grew up in Middlebury, Vermont. Attended Middlebury Union High School and graduated in 2014; began attending Tufts University the following autumn. Currently enrolled at Tufts University, majoring in computer science through the School of Engineering. Member of the Tufts Wind Ensemble; has played trumpet for nearly ten years. Enjoys skiing, programming, video gaming, and composing and arranging music.
